# Calendar & Date Handling

> **Trigger keywords:** calendar, scheduling, day of week, date verification, morning briefing

---

## Critical Rule

**DO NOT trust the system-provided "Today's date" for day-of-week.**

The system date may show the correct date but wrong day of week.

---

## When Working with Calendars/Scheduling

1. Use an external source to verify day of week if critical
2. Or calculate: Jan 1, 2026 = Wednesday, count forward
